About Wetro Compo site

We deliver the future

Wetro Composite Industries L.L.C (WCI) is a leading FRP manufacturing company with over 30 years of experience. We specialize in designing, manufacturing, and installing various FRP products, including tanks, vessels, ducts, pipes, and fittings. Our expertise lies in working with materials such as fiberglass, PVC-FRP, CPVC-FRP, PP-FRP, PPH-FRP, PVDF-FRP, EPOXY, PHENOLIC, and FURANE. With our extensive experience, we are capable of manufacturing fiberglass tanks in compliance with various standards, including BS, ASTM, ASME, AD 2000-Merkblatt, and customer-specific requirements. Our piping solutions cover a wide range of industries, such as Power & Desalination, Firewater, Water and Wastewater Treatment, Chemical Plants, and District Cooling.

We offer corrosion-resistant GRP ducting, bends, tees, flanges, reducers, dampers, and other accessories suitable for various applications. In addition to above-ground and underground fiberglass tanks, we also produce tanks for storing fuels like gasoline, aviation fuel, gasohol, jet fuel, diesel fuel, potable water, and wastewater at ambient underground temperatures. With our extensive industry experience and commitment to quality, WCI has earned a reputation as a trusted provider of FRP solutions.
