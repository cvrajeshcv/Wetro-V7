Infinite Possibilites with Wetro Composite

We Wetro Composite Industries L.L.C, are a leading FRP manufacturing company, engaged in the design, manufacture, supply, installation, and site jointing of various items made of FRP (GRP), PVC-FRP, CPVC-FRP, PP-FRP, PPH-FRP, PVDF-FRP, EPOXY, PHENOLIC, FURANE, etc.   

Fiberglass is an immensely versatile material due to its light weight, inherent strength, weather-resistant finish, and variety of surface textures. Qualities like corrosion resistance, non-conductivity, malleability, and durability, etc., make it the ideal material to be used in different industries. Fiberglass Reinforced Plastics, also known as Glass Reinforced Plastics is a modern composite material of construction for chemical plant equipment like Tanks & vessels, Ducts & Components, Pipes & Fittings, etc.  

Process Equipment and Pollution Control Equipment that are seamless molded structures of FRP with various grades of resins PP, PP-H, PVC, CPVC, PVDF, ECTFE, FEP, and PFA are Manufactured at WCI by contact molding and filament winding in compliance with international standards like BS 4994, BS EN13121, ASME RTP-1, ASME Section-X, ASTM D (3299, 4097, 3517, 3982, 4167, 2634, 3982).   

At WCI, fiberglass tanks can be designed and manufactured in accordance with BS, ASTM & ASME standards, AD 2000-Merkblatt as well as other customer-required standards.   

Also, WCI undertakes complete piping for plants like Power & Desalination, Firewater, Water and Waste Water Treatment, Chemical Plants, District cooling, etc. consisting of a wide range of classifications that are designed for pressure ratings ranging from gravity (3bar) to 32 bars with diameters from 25mm to 4500mm can be produced to suit project requirements. Similarly, WCI produces a complete range of ducting, bends, tees, flanges, reducers, dampers, and other accessories fabricated in corrosion-resistant GRP taking care of mild waste air ductwork to aggressive chemical duct handling caustic or sulphuric acid systems and elevated temperature services.   

Single-walled or double-walled models insulated or not, in diameter up to 4.5m of above-ground fiberglass tanks along with saddles, legs, or a skirt, and underground tanks used to store fuels such as gasoline, aviation fuel, gasohol, jet fuel, diesel fuel, potable water and wastewater at ambient underground temperature are also manufactured at WCI.  

Areas We Excel

The magnificent corrosion resistant property of FRP products are likely to be the ideal solution in marine industry. Wetro products like FRP Scrubber, FRP Pipes and Fittings, Ballast water treatment FRP tanks are widely used in offshore sector.

Oil & Gas

– FRP products Such as FRP Tanks and Pipelines plays an inevitable part in oil and gas sector. The non-corrosive characteristics boost its demand to almost all the nook and corners.
